ahegao

ahegao is the answer to the question, "What if every picture on the internet was replaced with anime girls?"
In short, every picture on a website is replaced with anime ~

Platforms:
Chrome extension

Date of birth:
February 9th, 2019

I saw the LEGO Movie 2 and thought it was really fun how the characters would just build to their heart's content without worrying about whether or not the project would be useful. After the movie, I wanted to build something for the sake of my own content.
While coming up with ideas, the question at the top of this page entered my mind...

The rest is history ????
