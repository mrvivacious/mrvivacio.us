I found myself sometimes spending excess time trying to decide what Magical Message to send to a loved one.

This tool, meant to enable quick connection between loved ones, was being used not-so-quickly as I'd continue generating messages until I found a message that I finally felt was appropriate.

Response: An Alexa skill for which all I have to do is to specify a recipient. Alexa will pick a message of its own accord (Math.random) and then text the recipient with that message.

Super quick.

Super magical.

Magical Messages.
