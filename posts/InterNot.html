InterNot!

One idea for helping people overcome porn and its effects is to spend more time outside with other people.

I came up with this idea in response to frustration while developing PorNo!. Although it is an idea right now, InterNot! was a cop-out at the time of its origin.

My friend suggested making an app "InterNot!" that disabled one's internet after I shared this thought with him.

It's a funny suggestion, has an effective implementation, and was a bit tenacious to debug.

InterNot!.

Here is the source code. Install it on your friends' computers, right-click the extension (orange heart) and select "Hide in Chrome Menu" to play a funny prank/lose your friends.
